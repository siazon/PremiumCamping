About Us

Premium Camping

Premium Camping is a family run business which provides camping facilities at a camp site in the west of Ireland. In conjunction with its traditional offering of pitches and mobile homes Premium Camping will also offer the option of booking wooden lodges and tree houses. From July 2022. we have included additional services, bringing to the table a unique set of packages. Our locations mean you are truly amongst the forest, eating, sleeping and playing in them every day, and that is our main vision. We believe camping allows for a rewardingly simple lifestyle that brings you back to your natural habitat. We aim to make camping accessible for all. By improving knowledge, offering advice where possible and providing a range of attractive sites with the right facilities, we can do our bit to make camping accessible. Camping is fun because it's an experience. Not only do you engage with nature and wildlife, you're also leaving behind the stress and hustle of city life. Get the campfire roaring, and learn about the world around you, you're making memories that will last a lifetime.

Fun facts about camping
  • The world's smallest camper is called a "cramper." It is pedal powered and has 4 feet of living space.
  • Crickets can calculate the temperature.
  • Raccoons may sound like cats, but you shouldn't cuddle them.
  • The founder of modern recreational camping was Thomas Hiram Holding, who wrote the first edition of The Camper's Handbook in 1908.
